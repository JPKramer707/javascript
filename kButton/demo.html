kButton Demo

Turns any image (or, with easy modification, any element) into a button. The button can have up and down images, and can function as a toggle or momentary-contact. Events can be attached to fire on click, on toggle down, or on toggle up.

The biggest job that this object does is to manage your button's visual display and fire events for you.

Note that even when a button is functioning in toggle mode, it will still fire the onClick event (if any is given) alongside the onToggleDown or onToggleUp events
