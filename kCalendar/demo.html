kCalendar/kDatePicker/kCalSet Demo

This is actually a composite demo.

kCalendar
The core calendar-generator used by both kDatePicker and kCalSet. It can also be used by itself.
kDatePicker
Reacts to tags written with a class of "date," example: <input type="text" class="date" value="July 13, 1980" /> Attaches to them a popup date picker.
kCalSet
Creates a set of calendars which permit such things as date selection and scrolling through the calendar list to reach further months/years.
